If the Office is Closed
In case of an urgent problem outside our office hours, one of our doctors is on-call 24 hours a day.
For urgent problems, the doctor on call can be reached by calling the usual office number, 253-581-2111.
Please do not use the Emergency call for routine questions.
Please call, rather than going first to another emergency facility or worrying yourself through a crisis.
For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 for immediate medical help.

Planning Your Visit
Everybody knows that visiting the doctor can be a time consuming affair.
Here at Lakewood Pediatrics, we try out best to minimize your wait time.
One way to speed up the process is to have the necessary paperwork out of the way.
What Can You Do?
Please download and complete the Patient Registration Form and the Privacy Practices Form if at least one of the following applies to you:
  • You are a new patient
  • You have not filled out these forms in the last year
  • Your information has changed
Also, please remember to bring your insurance card with you to your visit.
Please remember that children under 14 years old must be accompanied by an adult whose name is listed on the Privacy Practices Form.
